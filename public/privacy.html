Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies

Our digital service employs cookies and analogous tracking mechanisms to elevate your user interaction, evaluate platform engagement, and customize displayed content. Cookies are minute data packets saved on your device during your platform usage.

We utilize both temporary session cookies, which vanish upon browser closure, and enduring persistent cookies, which persist on your device for a predefined duration. These technologies may gather details like device specifications, browsing patterns, and other pertinent information.

You retain the ability to deactivate cookies through your web browser's configuration. Be advised, however, that disabling cookies might impair the functionality of certain platform features.
